#♂️ Relic's Curse

**Relic's Curse** is a 3D narrative-driven adventure game where you play as a hero on a mission to retrieve a sacred relic and save a tribe from a corrupted wizard.

---

##  How to Play

###  Objective
- Talk to the quest-giving villager to begin your quest.
- Sneak past the wizard’s patrolling guards—don’t get caught!
- Dodge fireballs cast by the wizard while trying to survive.
- Retrieve the relic and return it to the villager to complete your mission.

---

##  Controls

| Action         | Key        |
|----------------|------------|
| Move           | WASD       |
| Look Around    | Mouse      |
| Jump           | Spacebar   |
| Interact/Talk  | E          |

---

##  Gameplay Features

- NPCs with patrol paths and detection zones.
- Fade-to-black + teleport when caught by guards.
- Wizard boss that casts HP-draining fireballs.
- Quest tracker with live updates.
- Animated pop-up when completing the quest.

---

##  Assets Used

- Unity Starter Assets – Third Person Controller  
- Mixamo animations  
- Polygonal Low Poly Particle Pack (Rain FX)  
- Sound effects from [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/)

##  How to Run

1. Open the project in **Unity 2022.3 LTS or newer**.
2. Open the `MainScene` located in the `Scenes` folder.
3. Click **Play** in the Unity Editor to test the game.

> Make sure the NavMesh is baked and audio clips are properly assigned in the Inspector.
